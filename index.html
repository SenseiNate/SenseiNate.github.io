// About

No bootcamp origin story. Ten years of high-consequence work, now pointed at AI.

I came up in the Air Force as a munitions systems specialist. I tracked 6.6 million pieces of sensitive inventory across 57 accounts with zero discrepancies, ran an 18-person team over a $73M asset portfolio, held a 100% quality-assurance pass rate for seven straight years, and directed a $7.9B special operations fleet. When the margin for error is zero, you learn to build systems that don't fail.

Then I carried that into engineering. At Northrop Grumman I worked the Sentinel ICBM modernization program using MBSE, Cameo, and IBM DOORS, chaired 20+ TRBs and JTRBs, rebuilt a legacy compliance framework cutting cycle time 50%, and ran a full FMEA that surfaced 15 critical single points of failure. I also directed an $8M emergency deployment at 100% on-time. At GE Healthcare I shipped an automated onboarding engine cutting clinical verification from 5 days to 4 hours, reconciled 208K records to clear a $20M discrepancy with zero errors, reduced unauthorized access events 95%, and delivered the OEC Elite 3D needle trajectory feature to zero post-release issues and global physician adoption.

At BAE Systems I'm the primary USG point of contact on a $60M+ VTSN program spanning Johns Hopkins APL, Utah SDL, Northrop Grumman Prime, and Space Force installations, and simultaneously leading independent USG SE oversight on a $60M+ LEN contested integration, protecting government financial and programmatic interests before acceptance. I led the foundational data strategy for the Axiom/Wasatch enterprise cloud hybrid platform, shipped an internal LLM tool saving engineers 10+ hours a week, prevented over $500K in misallocated budget, built a 200+ engineer information portal, and cut programmatic overhead 60%. Made senior lead in 60 days. Same discipline, new tools. Non-traditional founder, tattoos and a TS clearance included.
