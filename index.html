// About

No bootcamp origin story. Ten years of high-consequence work, now pointed at AI.

I came up in the Air Force as a munitions systems specialist. I tracked 6.6 million pieces of sensitive inventory across 57 accounts with zero discrepancies, ran an 18-person team over a $73M asset portfolio, and held a 100% quality-assurance pass rate for seven straight years. When the margin for error is zero, you learn to build systems that don't fail.

Then I carried that into engineering. At Northrop Grumman I rebuilt a legacy compliance framework and automated it, cutting cycle time in half. At GE Healthcare I shipped an automated onboarding engine that took client verification from five days to four hours, plus a data pipeline that reconciled 208,000 records to clear a $20M discrepancy with zero errors.

At BAE Systems I build the AI. I shipped an internal LLM tool that killed manual documentation work and gave engineers back 10+ hours a week, cut programmatic overhead 60%, and made senior lead inside 60 days. Same discipline, new tools. Non-traditional founder, tattoos and a TS clearance included. I'd rather show you the work than talk about it.
